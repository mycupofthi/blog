Setting A Mother’s Day Brunch Table With Crumb

Hosting Mother’s Day Brunch can induce a smidge of intimidation in even the most seasoned home entertainers. I mean, who do we really want to impress more than our own mothers? On a day that’s about showing all the love to mom, it’s nice to have a few tricks up your sleeve that will impress, but also keep you stress-free when you’re prepping the house for guests. We’ve asked the creator of Crumb, Devin Connell, to share her top tips for setting a meaningful Mother’s Day table.
